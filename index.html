About Me

Hey there! I'm a tech enthusiast with a diverse background that includes film/TV, acting, and even some stunt work. But above all, I'm passionate about coding, anime, and video games.

My love for coding started in my teens when I designed custom MySpace layouts for bands. Since then, I've gained hands-on experience in web development and design, including building websites for restaurants during the Flash era.

Fast forward to today, and I've completed a full-stack bootcamp, the prestigious Harvard CS50 course, and Meta's front-end web developer program. I'm currently sharpening my skills with the Codecademy full-stack program and always seeking to expand my knowledge.

In addition to my coding prowess, I'm also proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Audition, Illustrator, and Premiere, making media-related editing a breeze for me. So whether it's coding or editing, I'm ready to bring my unique perspective and attention to detail to any project.

I'm excited about the endless possibilities in the tech industry, and I'm committed to becoming a proficient and confident developer who can bring innovation to the table. When I'm not coding, you can find me watching anime or exploring new video games. Let's create some amazing things together!
