Hi! I'm Christopher Quiogue.

I'm a software engineer coming from the film / TV world.

As a tech professional with a diverse background, I bring a unique perspective to the world of coding. With 15 years of experience in film/TV, working on camera, editing, and as a talent (actor/stunt performer), I've honed my skills in creativity, problem-solving, and attention to detail. I've always been passionate about coding and have pursued it as a hobby alongside my work in the entertainment industry. From creating customized MySpace layouts for bands in my teens to building websites for restaurants during the Flash era, I've gained hands-on experience in web development and design.

Now, I'm taking my coding skills to the next level. I've completed a full-stack bootcamp with Nucamp, the prestigious Harvard CS50 course, and Meta's front-end web developer program. Currently, I'm furthering my knowledge through the Codecademy full-stack program, constantly seeking to improve and expand my skillset. In addition to my coding prowess, I'm proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Audition, Illustrator and Premiere, making media-related editing a breeze for me. I'm eager to leverage my creativity, technical skills, and extensive experience in the entertainment industry to create innovative and cutting-edge solutions.

I'm excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in the tech industry, and I'm committed to becoming a proficient and confident developer who can bring unique perspectives to any project. Let's create some amazing things together!
